  • ORIGIN: LA DEMOCRACIA, HUEHUETENANGO, GUATEMALA
    VARIETY:BOURBON AND CATURRA
    PROCESS: WASHED
    NOTES:BROWN SUGAR, NECTARINE, BISCUIT COOKIE

    Rodulfo Jacinto López’s farm in La Democracia is at the end of a long winding road that leads to his beautiful home which is surrounded by pine trees and flowers native to Guatemala. Rodulfo has over 30 years experience farming coffee and eventually he plans to hand over the responsibilities of the family farm to his three sons. He produces great quality coffee year after year it is said that his high energy and contagious laugh will forever be embodied in his coffee for many years to come.

    We are proud to offer his coffee as part of our line up and love the warm flavour profile of this delicious Guatemalan coffee.
